Understanding Your Results

Your HealthScore is calculated based on globally recognized health metrics. BMI is a key indicator of body fatness and overall health risk.

BMI Categories:

  • Below 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9: Healthy Weight
  • 25.0 – 29.9: Overweight
  • 30.0 and Above: Obese

Why Monitoring Calories Matters?

To maintain your current weight, your body needs a specific amount of energy (calories). If you want to lose weight, aim for a "Calorie Deficit" by eating slightly less than your result.
